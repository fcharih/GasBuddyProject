Help us annotate gas prices

Instructions

To earn your reward, you are to give us the price of regular gas for each photo. In the input boxes, put the prices in numerical form. The prices should be in the form "x.xx" where "x" is a number or a dash (if the number is not available or unclear).

Special cases

Case 1: Price for regular gas is not available
Enter in "-.--".
Case 2: Some of the figures are ambiguous/unclear
Replace these ambiguous figures with dashes, for e.g. "3.-5".
Case 3: There are fractions in the price (e.g. 8/10)
Ignore fractions.
